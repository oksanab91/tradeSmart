To the Trade Smart!

The Trade Smart system is a new, self-contained product intended for use on the Internet and Android platform.

The mobile application allows Conestoga students and College community members to search for the property they want to buy or rent.

Trade Smart provides Conestoga community members with the barcode scanner to scan products QR code and obtain full details of the ads.
